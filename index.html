Who We Are

We are a small group of people who strive to improve the lives of children in poverty. We have taken a step by running a Residential Balakashram for 25 years at Yeoor Hills in a natural atmosphere in Thane city. Accommodating initially 60 to 70 such children who are either having a single parenthood or have none to care of.

What We Do

There are numbers of undernourished, poor, orphaned, destitute, homeless, adivasis children around looking to us with whirling eyes and stretched hands for help. The Ashram provides them with residence, food, schooling, health care, clothing and all such facilities to bring them up parallel to those who are fortunate to get this all at their home.
